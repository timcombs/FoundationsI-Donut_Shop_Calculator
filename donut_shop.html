Donut Baking Calculator

Instructions for the donut baking calculator

The donut shop calculator is intended to help you plan how many donuts to bake each hour for your specific store.

The calculations are based on:
the amount of customers that come in per hour
the average amount of donuts each customer purchases
the hours of operation

Please click on the donut under your store name.

After table loads, you may change the Average Donuts per Purchase value.
Doubleclick in the cell, enter the new amount and press enter.
